What is drawing?

Drawing is an artform that is created usually using a writing utensil such as a pencil or pen and dragging it against a surface such as paper, creating lines. Drawings are usually where most people start for creating art in different mediums. (EX: If you were going to paint a picture, usually you would sketch it out before painting.)

How do I get started?

The great thing about drawing is that you don't really need to go out and buy a bunch of specialized materials to practice. Most people already have pencils (or another writing utensil) around their house as well as some paper. The main key to becoming a skilled in drawing is to never stop practicing and looking for ways to improve. There are also plenty of resources if you look around- websites like YouTube have tutorials if you don't feel like leaving the house, or if you have an art center in your local area- most of the time they offer basic art classes to teach drawing techniques.

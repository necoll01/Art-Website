What is Painting?

Painting is a two-dimensional art form that uses a pigment across a flat surface to create an image, most commonly with paint and a paint brush. There are three general kinds of paint: acrylic, oil, and watercolor. The kind you choose to pursue is up to you, but the easiest to start with is probably acrylic. It is cheap, readily available, and dries quickly.

How do I get started?

I always recommend reading up on any new hobby you start, or watching videos. But once you have done your own research it is best to head down to your local art shop and get yourself a student-level paint set for which ever kind you decide is best for you. Canvas that is pre-primed works best after you have chosen your paints. Just make sure not to hit it with any sharp edges, canvas dents easy. Go slow and have fun. Practice is the biggest factor in artistic success.

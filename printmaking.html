Linocut Print by Natalie Collins (2015)

What is printmaking?

Printmaking is defined as the art of making prints. One of the most accessible form of printmaking for modern artists is called linocut. Linocut is done by carving a sheet of linoleum with a linocut tool. The parts that you do not want to show up on the paper you must carve deeply so that part of the linoleum does not touch the paper when you essentially stamp it onto paper. After you are done carving you must roll ink onto your linoleum with an ink roller and stamp it onto paper evenly.

How do I start printmaking?

The easiest way to start printmaking is to watch a few tutorials to get a better idea for the entire process, and then head down to your local art store and pickup some linoleum, a linocut tool, printmaking ink, paper and an ink roller. The employees at the art store should be able to help you decide which tools are best for you as a brand new printmaker. Just remember to have fun and carve away from yourself!

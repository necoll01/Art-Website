If you were to want to try all of these methods of visual art out...

A combination of all of these tools should give you a decent start into the world of visual art! If you are on a budget though, maybe mix and match. You would be surprised how often you can use things that aren't necessarily made for art on a piece!

Supply Recommendation Chart
Drawing Painting Sculpting Printmaking
Pencil Canvas Carving Tools Linoleum
Sketchbook Paint Brushes Clay Linocut Tool
Eraser Tubes of Paint Play-doh Ink

There may be people who would say you should buy the best of these things, but you will break tools, and who knows if you will even like some of these things? I personally would save bigger investments for down the road! Something I wish someone would've told me is that getting the highest possible quality of art supplies doesn't improve skill.
